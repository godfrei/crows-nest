Level Reviews A-L

To read the review on a particular level, just select it from the list below. The list will be updated as new levels appear, and as I get time to go back and review the older ones. The date given is when the first review for the level was posted. Percentage Ratings are listed with the author of the review for quick reference. If you want to find out more on the ratings, you can check the percentage scale in the Database. There's more information on my personal rating style in there as well.

All levels reviewed by me are run on a 5x86/133 PC, with 16M of RAM, Quad Speed CD-ROM and a 2M video card. Other reviewers have different equipment, but I'll try to include any hardware requirements the level authors give, such as RAM minimums, suggested processors or video cards, in the Tech Specs at the end of each review.

All the levels are listed in alphabetical order; the order of listing should in no way represent the quality of the individual levels. If you have comments, a review of your own to add to this collection, know of a new level or have one you'd like reviewed, mail me.

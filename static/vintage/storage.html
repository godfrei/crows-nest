Here you can find all the components that can be used to take your adventures to the next plateau. You can choose from the many missions of Kyle Katarn, download new sounds, WAXes, 3DOs and lots more. This area will be kept fairly up to date thanks to the efforts of all the people who keep me informed of new items. A special thanks also goes to Bryant Luk of swgamers.com, who has graciously allowed me to link to his file directories. If you have new add-ons and want them posted for download, you can mail it to me and I'll review it and send it on, or send it to Bryant. If you decide to send it to Bryant please can drop me a quick line so I know it's there. Otherwise I'll proabably get to it in a few days.

All files are in alphabetical order by filename, if only because it's easier to organize then shifting by title. This seems to be the way everyone's used to viewing them as well.

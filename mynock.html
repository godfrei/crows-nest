Mynock Commando Base

 Author: Douglas E. Haynes


Plot? What plot? There are no briefings included and no clues as to what your mission objectives are. At the end of the "level" you find deathstar plans or something, so I guess your objective is to get those plans.

Review by: Steve Noall


Since this "level" is basically just some square rooms connected with a few doors with a few bad guys here and there, I don't think I can say that much. The name of the level doesn't really make that much sense, and neither does anything in it. Besides the poor placement of the Dark Troopers, there are even a few funny looking Boba Fetts flying around. The texturing was not new, and actually very old and boring. The enemy placement is terrible, and halfway into the level, you're given practically every single weapon. There is no plot whatsoever, but I wouldn't expect someone to spend a great deal of time on a plot for a level like this.


There is nothing more I can say without making this a walk-through of the level. My advice to you is to avoid this mission completely.
